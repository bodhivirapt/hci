Topic case 1 - The no-Phone Phone

With the restrictions in mind, there is a way for a glove type interactions to be made to resemble a numpad. Using a combination of vibrations, micro computers, a tiny speaker and sim card. To get a better picture of the device, a sketch of it can be found below.





The users interact by holding pressure to each touch zone, when a number is selected the device will vibrate, embedded LED will shows the number chosen in the userâ€™s palm. As soon as the user finished dialling the number, the user can bring his hand to his ear and make a phone call.

The sensors involved are pressure plates and microphones to capture touch and audio along with speakers and LED as its main output.

The glove would be created from a thin and breathable mesh fabric material with different skin tones available. This creates a non-intrusive look. The glove should fit the 4 fingers leaving the thumb open to be its main controller.
