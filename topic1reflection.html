What did you think of this topic?

I am really interested in how playful interactions can influence a day to day activities of people interacting with it. Although, I could also see how this type of interactions especially in public could be a source of irritation. Therefore, I think there are should be multiple considerations in mind when implementing playful interactions. Examples of these considerations are:

  • Interactions should not disturb people using it on a day to day basis
  • Interactions should be designed thoroughly to prevent user frustration
  • Some interactions are meant to be more utilitarian and not playful


    • How do you see applications and the future of this?

    In an ironic twist, playful interactions could be used to reduce stimulants in people on a day to day basis. People playing their phones while waiting on the elevator could instead interact in a playful experience which does not require being mentally overloaded by everything on their mobile devices. Besides that, i think playful interactions may also be a great gateway for people with mental disabilities to adapt into norm social behaviours.
