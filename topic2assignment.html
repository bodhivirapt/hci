New XR Concept - Audio Buddy



For this concept I wanted to tackle a concept for healthcare. Specifically, adding XR into people with visual impairment. This is because even on 2022 there is still a gap in accessibility for the visually impaired.

In this concept, I was thinking of headphones, similar to hearing aids but are able parse sentences where the head of the user is facing at a click of a button or gesture. This will allow a visual impaired person to read something far away without the touching it like braille.

But how would the user know if there is any text in the first place? Similar to haptic touch on an iPhone, small non intrusive vibrations will notify the users every-time there is a text available to parse and read.
