Muse Headpiece

For the research section of this assignment I will be talking about the Muse Headband. Muse is a company founded by InteraXon a Canadian based innovator in the field of brain sensing.





The muse headband is an impressive piece of wearable technology that utilises sensors that capture EEG signals from the brain. The EEG signals are then parsed by the software into understandable piece of information that the user can understand.

This piece of technology is mainly used in meditations to help calm the mind. The benefit of this real-time technology allows users to use real time feedback to help guide your meditation more accurately instead of using an app that does not make use of the users actual brain activity.
